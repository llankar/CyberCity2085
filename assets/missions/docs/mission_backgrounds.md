# Mission Backgrounds

This folder contains 60 mission background images for CyberCity 2085:

- 30 cyber city backgrounds in `backgrounds/city/`
- 30 wasteland backgrounds in `backgrounds/wasteland/`

All images are JPG files normalized to `1600x1200` for 4:3 mission presentation. They were made as text-mission backplates, so the scenes avoid foreground characters and intentional readable text, logos, numbers, UI, and watermarks.

Use `background_manifest.json` as the integration source. It records stable IDs, categories, relative paths, dimensions, and short mood notes for mission authors.

Generation decisions:

- City scenes mix interiors and exteriors so missions can cover infiltration, care, transit, surveillance, border, and safehouse beats.
- Wasteland scenes mix open travel routes, ruins, shelters, hazardous infrastructure, and small survival spaces.
- The set favors evocative but restrained environment art so mission text remains the emotional focus.
- Images were post-processed to exact JPG dimensions rather than relying on generator output size.
- The `21`-`30` additions lean into 2077-era cyberpunk lore archetypes such as corporate enclaves, netrunner dens, combat zones, nomad camps, extraction rigs, and badlands smuggling routes while staying original to CyberCity 2085.
